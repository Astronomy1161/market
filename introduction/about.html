About Aquatrade

Who We Are

Aquatrade is an online marketplace designed to connect local fishermen directly with buyers. Our goal is to create a platform where fresh seafood can be traded efficiently, transparently, and safely. We support local fishermen by giving them an easier way to market their catches while helping buyers access high-quality seafood at fair prices.

Our Mission

To empower local fishermen by providing a modern digital platform that supports fair trade, transparency, and accessibility for both sellers and buyers.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital seafood marketplace in the region, revolutionizing how fresh catch is bought and sold while uplifting coastal communities.
